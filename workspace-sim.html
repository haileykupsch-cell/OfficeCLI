KB-5086 — Idle Case SLA Policy
Updated: 31 May 2026 Author: Ops Policy Team Version: 3.2

Overview

Cases with no activity for ≥48 hours are classified as Idle. This policy defines SLA obligations and required actions for idle case management.

⚠️ As of v3.2, the idle threshold was reduced from 72h to 48h. All existing automation rules have been updated.

SLA Tiers

48h idle → Flag + 2h response SLA
60h idle → Auto-escalate, 30m SLA
72h idle → Director notification required

Required Actions

  • Assign owner within 30 minutes of idle flag
  • Send customer update per SOP-4821 template
  • Log reason for idle state in CRM notes field
  • Set next-touch reminder ≤2h from current time

Escalation Path

If no owner available → use escalation path per SOP-4821 (Escalation Path Delta). Auto-assign to queue lead after 15 minutes unacknowledged.
